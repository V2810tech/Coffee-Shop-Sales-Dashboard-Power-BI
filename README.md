# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Internship Task 2
This project was completed as **Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling** for my **Data Analyst Internship**.

### 🎯 Objective
To create an interactive Power BI dashboard that presents meaningful business insights from a sales dataset using effective data visualization and storytelling techniques.

---

## 📖 Project Overview
This dashboard analyzes Coffee Shop sales data to help understand revenue trends, product performance, customer payment preferences, and weekly sales patterns. The report focuses on business insights that support better decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- 💰 Total Revenue KPI
- 🧾 Total Transactions KPI
- ☕ Coffee Varieties KPI
- 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
- ☕ Revenue by Coffee Product
- 🏆 Top 5 Selling Coffee Products
- 💳 Payment Method Distribution
- 📅 Revenue by Day of Week
- 📅 Date Range Filter
- 💳 Payment Method Filter
- 📄 Executive Summary Page

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights
- March recorded the highest sales.
- Latte generated the highest revenue.
- Card payments accounted for approximately 95% of all transactions.
- Sales remained relatively stable throughout the week.
- The dashboard helps identify top-performing products and customer payment behavior.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Promote best-selling coffee products through combo offers.
- Increase marketing during lower-performing periods.
- Continue encouraging digital payment methods.
- Improve promotions for lower-performing products.
